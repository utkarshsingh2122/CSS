1) Block level elements :- Start from a new line and take complete width
Margin is in every direction .
Padding can be also done in every direction .
To convert Block level element into Inline element we will use (display : inline).
2) Inline elements :- Start from same line and take only required width
Margin can only be added in left or right .
Padding can be done from top to bottom but it will mess up
Example of Block level elemments are :-
heading(1-6) , p , div ,ul ,li ,form , article , aside ,main , nav etc.
Example of Inline elements are:-
span , img , a , textarea etc.
